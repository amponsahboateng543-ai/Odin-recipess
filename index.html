What is Boulding?

Borlding is a form of rock climbing that is performed on small rock formations or artificial rock walls without the use ropes or harnesses.

unlike traditional climbing, boulding force on short but intensive routes called "problem,"typically keeping climbers within a safe distance from the ground while they navigate challenging sequences of moves.

Experience the perfect blend of physical challenge and mental puzzle-solving as you navigate routes close to the ground, with nothing but a crash pad and determination. Boulding strips climbing down to its purest from, making it both accessible to beginners and endlessly challenging for experts.

Why Boulding?

whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced athlete, climbers of all ages can challenge themselves, celebrate each other's successes, and build lasting friendships.
